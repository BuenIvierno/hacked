Welcome to Hacked!

In the last decade cases of cybercrime have increased exponentially.
The World Economic Forum warns us that ransomware attacks and other forms of cybercrime are one of the primary risks to take into account in 2023.
While it might seem like cybercriminals might have nothing to do with you, the first step towards being safe on the internet is to take cybersecurity seriously!
Cybercriminals are looking for ways to make quick and easy money from people's private information, so don't make it easy for them to access your digital spaces!
