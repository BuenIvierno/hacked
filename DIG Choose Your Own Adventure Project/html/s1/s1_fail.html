Using the same password for multiple sites, or even storing your passwords in a vulnerable non-encrypted location might be a risk factor when navigating digital spaces.
Using a password, again and again, means that your accounts might be vulnerable to data leaks or malicious actors trying to access your account on a website with low-security measures.
This could put your personal email or even your bank account at risk. A password manager is a great way to keep your digital information safe.
Good password managers encrypt all your passwords in an encrypted server only accessible by a master password that you create, this makes it way harder for cybercriminals to access your information!
