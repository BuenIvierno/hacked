Remember, to be safer on the internet you should
  1. Find a password manager that works for you and create a strong master password
  2. Make sure you keep your devices updated, developers are constantly working to make your devices more resilient against cybercrime
  3. Use a VPN whenever you connect to a public network, it is important to keep your traffic secure and private
  4. Back up the data of your mobile devices. They store information about you than you don't even know about!
  5. Beware phishing scams. These often look very convincing and if not taken seriously could become a huge problem for you and the people close to you!
